Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CodeFront Studio ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit codefrontstudio.com (the "Site") or purchase our products.

1. Who we are

CodeFront Studio is operated by Muhammad Ali Usman, an individual developer, based in Pakistan. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at codefrontgames@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly — such as your email address, if you contact us or sign up for updates.
  • Purchase information — when you buy a product, payment processing is handled entirely by our merchant of record, Lemon Squeezy. We do not directly collect or store your payment card details. Lemon Squeezy's own privacy policy governs how they handle that information.
  • Basic technical data — such as browser type, general location (country-level), and pages visited, collected automatically through standard web server and hosting logs (via our hosting provider, Vercel).

3. How we use your information

  • To deliver the products or services you've purchased
  • To respond to your questions or support requests
  • To send you updates about products, only if you've opted in
  • To maintain and improve the Site's security and performance

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Third parties we work with

We rely on the following third-party services, each of which processes certain data under their own privacy policies:

  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing and merchant of record services
  • Vercel — website hosting and infrastructure
  • Payoneer — used on our end for receiving payouts; this does not involve your data as a customer

5. Your rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or UK, you have rights under the GDPR, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your personal data, and the right to data portability. You can exercise these rights by contacting us directly.

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the CCPA/CPRA, including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

Regardless of location, you can always contact us to ask what information we hold about you or to request its deletion.

6. Data retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide our products and services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

7. Cookies

The Site itself does not use tracking or advertising cookies. Our hosting and payment providers may use essential cookies or similar technologies necessary for their services to function (e.g., checkout sessions).

8. Children's privacy

Our products and Site are not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. International data transfers

As we and our service providers operate internationally, your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We rely on our providers' compliance with applicable data protection frameworks for these transfers.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
