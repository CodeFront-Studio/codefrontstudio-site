About

Built by one developer, not a team

CodeFront Studio is a one-person shop. No roadmap decided by committee, no bloated feature creep — just one developer, building fast and shipping tools that solve real, specific problems.

Three years of self-taught coding across Python, JavaScript, and C# — started as a hobby, stayed one. Along the way: game AIs, small automation scripts, and now, tools built for other developers.
